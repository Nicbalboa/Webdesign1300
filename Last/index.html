Introduction to the 2017 Toyota 86

The 2017 Toyota 86 is a thrilling, agile sports coupe designed to deliver pure driving excitement. Born from the collaboration between Toyota and Subaru, this lightweight rear-wheel-drive car offers an unmatched driving experience with its balanced performance, precise handling, and a sleek design that turns heads. Whether you're an enthusiast or a casual driver, the 86 is built to bring out the best in every journey.


Performance and Engine

  • Performance: 0-60 mph in about 6.2 seconds (manual), top speed around 140 mph.
  • Suspension: Independent front and rear suspension for a balanced ride.
  • Chassis: Lightweight design with a low center of gravity for superior handling.
  • Handling: Known for its sharp steering and rear-wheel-drive setup, making it a favorite among driving enthusiasts.

Technology and features

The 2017 Toyota 86 is equipped with a range of technology designed to keep you connected and entertained on the go. The standard 7-inch touchscreen display features Toyota’s Entune™ Audio system, offering Bluetooth® connectivity, a USB port, and access to your favorite music and apps. Apple CarPlay and Android Auto are available on higher trims, giving you seamless smartphone integration for calls, messages, and navigation.
Tech and Features

Safety

Safety is always a priority in Toyota vehicles, and the 86 is no exception. It comes standard with advanced safety features like a backup camera, stability control, and traction control. Additional options, like blind-spot monitoring and rear cross-traffic alert, can help provide an extra layer of confidence while driving.
